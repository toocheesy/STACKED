How to Play STACKED!

Welcome to STACKED!, a strategic card game where you capture cards to score points and outsmart your opponents!

Objective:

Be the first to reach the target score (set in settings) by capturing cards from the board.

How to Play:

  • Setup: Each player (you and two bots) is dealt 4 cards. 4 cards are placed on the board.
  • Your Turn: Drag cards to the play area to build a combo:
    • Pair Capture: Use a hand card to capture board cards of the same value. For 2-10, capture one matching card (e.g., a 5 captures a 5). For J, Q, K, capture all matching cards (e.g., a K captures all Kâ€™s on the board).
    • Sum Capture (2-10 only): Use a hand card and two board cards that sum to the hand cardâ€™s value to capture a third board card (e.g., a 9 with a 3 and 6 captures another 9). J, Q, K cannot be used in sums.
  • Submit Combo: Click "Submit Move" to capture. The play area will glow green if your combo is valid. You can make multiple combos per turn!
  • End Turn: Drag a hand card to the board to place it and end your turn.
  • Reset Play Area: Click "Reset Play Area" to return cards to their original positions.
  • Scoring: Number cards (2-9) are worth 5 points, 10/J/Q/K are worth 10 points, Aces are worth 15 points.
  • Game End: The game ends when the deck runs out. The player with the highest score wins!
