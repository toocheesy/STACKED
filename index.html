How to Play STACKED!

Welcome to STACKED!, a strategic card game where you capture cards to score points and outsmart your opponents!

Objective:

Be the first to reach the target score (set in settings) by capturing cards from the board.

How to Play:

  • Setup: Each player (you and two bots) is dealt 4 cards. 4 cards are placed on the board.
  • Your Turn: Drag cards to the play areas to build a combo:
    • Match Capture: Place a card in Base area, then matching cards in Match area. Must use hand + board cards.
    • Sum Capture: Place target card in Base area. Use Sum areas with cards that total the Base card value. Must use hand + board cards.
    • Multi-Capture: Use multiple Sum areas and/or Match area simultaneously for massive point combos!
  • Submit Combo: Click "Submit Move" to capture. Valid areas glow green. Invalid areas shake and turn red.
  • End Turn: Drag a hand card to the board to place it and end your turn.
  • Reset Play Area: Click "Reset Play Area" to return cards to their original positions.
  • Scoring: Cards worth points based on rarity. Bigger combos = bigger scores!
  • Last Combo Takes All: When deck is empty, whoever made the last capture gets all remaining board cards!

Tips:

  • Plan multi-captures for maximum points per turn!
  • Watch the turn indicator to track whose turn it is.
  • Use the hint button if you're stuck!
  • Save powerful cards for the endgame when "Last Combo Takes All" triggers!
